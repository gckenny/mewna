English

Mewna ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data Mewna collects, how it is used, and your rights. By using Mewna, you agree to this policy.

The short version

  • Mewna runs entirely on your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • We do not operate any servers. Your data is never sent to us.
  • Optional cloud sync uses your own iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit private database — even Apple cannot read it on our behalf.
  • No advertising. No analytics. No third-party trackers. No accounts.

What data Mewna touches

DataSourcePurposeStored where
Sleep stages, heart rate, HRV, oxygen, respiratory rateHealthKit (with your permission)Generate your sleep summaryOn-device only; never leaves the app
Motion (accelerometer)Core Motion (with your permission)Detect sleep / wake from phone-on-bedAggregated to 1-minute buckets, on-device
Sleep sessions, scores, notes, tagsGenerated by you using the appYour sleep historySwiftData on-device + your private iCloud (if iCloud signed in)
Notifications (bedtime reminder)If you opt inLocal reminderiOS UNUserNotificationCenter, on-device

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect any data that leaves your device. There is no Mewna server.
  • We do not require an account, email, or sign-in.
  • We do not embed advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties — we have no data to share.

iCloud backup

If you are signed in to iCloud and have iCloud Drive enabled, your sleep history is synced via Apple's CloudKit private database under your Apple ID. The data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. We have no access to your iCloud private database. You can disable iCloud sync any time in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud → Mewna.

Children's privacy

Mewna is intended for general audiences (rated 4+). It does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children, because it does not collect identifiable information from anyone.

Your rights

  • Access: All your data is on your device. Use Settings → Export data (JSON) to retrieve it.
  • Erasure: Settings → Erase all records will remove every sleep session locally and from your iCloud private database.
  • Portability: The exported JSON is human-readable and re-importable.

Changes

If we change this policy, the new version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. We will not retroactively change how previously collected data is used (we collect none, so this is a non-issue).
