A cat curled by your pillow.

Mewna is a tiny moon-cat who watches over you while you sleep. Place your iPhone by the pillow, or wear your Apple Watch — we'll quietly read your night and tell you what happened, in human words.

Cole-Kripke actigraphy + Apple Watch heart rate / HRV. No sign-up, no servers, no ads. Everything stays on your device and in your private iCloud.

貓咪陪你入眠,月亮為你解夢。

喵娜是一隻陪你入睡的 AI 月亮貓。把 iPhone 放在枕邊,或戴上 Apple Watch,我們會默默地讀懂你的每一夜,並用人話告訴你發生了什麼。
